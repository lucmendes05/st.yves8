I think I might disappear,
I’ll stop talking to people,
They’ll think I lost my mind and
Packed my things in a sack and waved goodbye,
I’ll curl up in a ball - do nothing but cry
Swim in the silence, learn to fly

Abandoned houses I called home,
Left to wither and die,
Betrayed like I betrayed their trust,
Narrowed the canals through my teeth,
Ground the faith to dust,
Just add water.
